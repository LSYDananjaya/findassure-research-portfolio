## Design Context

### Users
Academic reviewers, hiring managers, and technical peers evaluating the FindAssure research project. They are looking for clear, precise communication of complex system architecture (capture, match, protect) and an interface that inspires confidence and trust.

### Brand Personality
Clinical, rigorous, precise, reliable.
The tone is academic yet modern. It should feel like a high-end research lab's dashboard or a declassified blueprint of a secure system.

### Aesthetic Direction
A highly structured, "industrial-brutalist" mixed with refined editorial minimalism.
- **Theme**: Light mode default with a clean, surgical feel.
- **References**: High-end architecture portfolios, technical whitepapers, secure terminal UIs.
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS "slop" (soft glowing purple/blue gradients, bouncy animations, rounded soft cards).
- **Typography**: Highly legible, sharp, and structured. 

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration**: Every line, border, and color must serve to explain the system, not just look pretty.
2. **Precision in motion**: Animations should be fast, sharp, and decisive. No bouncy or elastic easing.
3. **Tinted neutrals**: Neutrals should feel grounded and cohesive (cool slates).
4. **Typographic hierarchy**: Rely heavily on font size, weight, and spacing rather than color to establish structure.
